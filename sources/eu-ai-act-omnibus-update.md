## Key Finding

The May 7 "AI Act Omnibus" agreement did **not** extend the August 2, 2026 deadline. Article 50 transparency obligations (chatbot disclosure + deepfake labeling) take effect on 8/2 with **zero grace period**. Only Article 50(2) machine-readable watermarking gets a 4-month grace period for systems already on the market before 8/2.
